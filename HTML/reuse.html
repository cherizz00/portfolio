10 Best DSA Courses in India [2025 Edition]

By Anjali Sharma
May 14, 2025
12 min read

Mastering Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA) is essential for cracking tech interviews in 2025. We've evaluated the leading DSA courses in India, focusing on features like live classes, mentorship, interview prep, and student outcomes. Our list ranks LogicMojo's DSA course at #1, followed by other popular programs (Scaler, Coding Ninjas, GeeksforGeeks, etc.) trusted by Indian learners.

Below, find a detailed ranking, a comparison table, and an FAQ section to help you choose the right DSA program for your career goals.

1. Ranking Criteria

We assessed each course based on these key factors:

  • Comprehensive curriculum: Coverage of both core and advanced DSA topics
  • Mode of instruction: Live interactive sessions vs. self-paced learning
  • Mentorship quality: 1:1 doubt sessions, career guidance from industry experts
  • Hands-on projects: Practical application of concepts through real-world projects
  • Placement support: Mock interviews, resume help, and referral networks
  • Student reviews: Verified success stories and testimonials
  • Value proposition: Overall cost vs. benefits offered

2. Top DSA Courses in India (2025)

2.1 LogicMojo – DSA + System Design (Live Course)

LogicMojo's flagship DSA course (7-month live program) tops our list for its depth and support. It covers DSA from basics to advanced, plus system design (HLD/LLD). Classes are live on weekends (3h each), with 1:1 mentorship and unlimited doubt sessions. Students work on real projects and get continuous feedback; one review highlights "assignments after every session" and "interactive live doubt clearance" to reinforce learning. The course fee is around ₹45,000 (with EMI options). LogicMojo claims high placement success – e.g. students landing 80 LPA offers.

Pros:

  • Expert mentors with FAANG/MAANG experience
  • Extensive live support with real-time doubt resolution
  • Lifetime access to course materials and updates
  • Comprehensive mock interviews modeled after top tech companies
  • Personalized resume guidance and career coaching

Cons:

  • Longer duration (7 months) requiring consistent commitment
  • Premium price point compared to self-paced alternatives

2.2 Scaler Academy (by InterviewBit) – DSA & Algorithms Program

Scaler (InterviewBit Academy) offers a rigorous DSA program geared to product-job interviews. Its industry-vetted curriculum includes fundamentals, advanced DSA (4.5 months), plus modules on SQL, system design and even GenAI. Courses are live and include 1:1 mentorship from veteran engineers, real-world projects, and an "aspirational network" of peers. Career support is strong – mock interviews, resume/profile building, and referrals are built into the program. Scaler's programs typically span ~10–12 months. Pricing is high (often ₹3–4 lakh for premium tracks), though scholarships/EMIs are available.

Pros:

  • Highly structured content with industry-relevant curriculum
  • Expert faculty from top tech companies
  • Intensive practice with competitive programming challenges
  • Strong placement network with direct company connections

Cons:

  • Very costly compared to other offerings
  • Competitive admission process
  • Requires serious dedication and time commitment

2.3 Coding Ninjas – DSA (CareerCamp & Premium Courses)

Coding Ninjas is well-known for placement-oriented courses. Their CareerCamp DSA (in collaboration with IIT Madras) is a 4-month live program. It includes 10+ mock interviews, resume reviews, profile building and access to a dedicated placement cell. Instructors cover core DSA topics plus contest programming. The total fee for such courses starts around ₹19,999. Coding Ninjas also offers longer "Ninja Career Camp" programs (6–8 months, ~₹30k–₹40k) with additional mentorship.

Pros:

  • Structured curriculum co-designed with IIT faculty
  • Active career services with personalized 1:1 mock interviews
  • Dedicated placement support team
  • Extensive practice problems and peer coding contests

Cons:

  • Primarily targeted at college students
  • Less hands-on mentoring than full bootcamps
  • Medium-range price (still a significant investment)

2.4 GeeksforGeeks – DSA Self-Paced Course

GeeksforGeeks (GfG) offers a self-paced DSA course that's hugely popular in India (100K+ students). It provides 60+ hours of recorded lectures (by GfG founder Sandeep Jain), 200+ practice problems, 10 coding contests, and 5+ DSA projects. Learners get 12 months of doubt support and multiple-choice quizzes. Topics cover everything from arrays and trees to advanced DP and segment trees. All this comes at an affordable price – roughly ₹3,899 (with optional ₹1,499 for 24×7 doubt resolution).

Pros:

  • Extremely affordable compared to live programs
  • Flexible learning schedule
  • Comprehensive content with completion certificate
  • Excellent for beginners and self-motivated learners

Cons:

  • 100% self-study without live classes
  • Limited mentor interaction
  • No personalized support beyon
